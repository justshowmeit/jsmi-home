Mobile Application Privacy Policy for Just Show Me It

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Just Show Me It for Android ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Just Show Me It ("we").The Application is an image generation tool which takes internet content and generates it into a summary image.

When was this Privacy Policy last updated?

23rd March 2016: added the paragraph mentioning very limited use of the Google Analytics service in the application.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application does not currently solicit user-entered information.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, transactional data related to the content shared via the Application, information about the way you use the Application and technical feedback of Application errors.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us improve the Application and our service. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

Application error information may be periodically transmitted to the Crashlytics service whose terms can be found at https://try.crashlytics.com/terms/ - continued use of the Application is deemed approval of the aforementioned Crashlytics terms.

Anonymized Application usage information including, but not limited to, user session frequency and user session length will be periodically sent to the Answers service, whose terms can be found at https://answers.io/privacy and at https://answers.io/terms - continued use of the Application is deemed approval of the aforementioned Answers terms.

The Application makes very basic use of the Google Analytics service in order to find out from where we attract new users and to see how the application is used in aggregate. None of the information collected in this way is personally identifiable and google's terms in fact forbids this. By using Google Analytics in the application, Just Show Me It has agreed to abide by their terms listed at www.google.com/analytics/terms/.

We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;

  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;

  • with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhereto the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

  • if Just Show Me It is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or aportion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of this information, as well as any choices you may have regarding this information.

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
